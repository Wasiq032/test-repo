Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain.
  6. Spread a bit of meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce.
  8. Top with shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
  10. Let cool for 15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
